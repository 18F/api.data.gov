api.data.gov

api.data.gov is a free API management service for federal agencies that serves to fulfill GSA's obligations under the Open Government Data Act of 2018 by offering an API point of entry to share government data with the public. Our aim is to make it easier for government to release and manage APIs and to date is used by 25 agencies for over 450 APIs.
